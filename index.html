Subhadip Satpati is a Computer Science student (MC(A) - cs) and technology enthusiast based in Contai, Purba Medinipur, West Bengal.

He founded Subhadip Cyber Technology (SCT) in 2025 to provide high-quality, affordable digital support to small and medium-sized businesses. From responsive landing pages to complex MERN stack applications, Subhadip brings technical precision and creative vision to every project.

Under his leadership, SCT offers end-to-end creative services spanning web/app development, video editing, and graphic design, ensuring clients receive top-tier technological solutions.
